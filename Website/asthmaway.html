The problem

Asthma is a long-term respiratory condition that causes wheezing, coughing and shortness of breath. It is prevalent in children, affecting more than 1 million children nationwide, but can be controlled by proper use of aerosolized medicines.

Every 10 seconds, someone has an asthma attack, but these need not be fatal. Most attacks are preventable with proper use of an inhaler and spacer, however most children are never taught how to use them properly.

The AsthmAway spacer

The AsthmAway spacer is the world's first smart inhaler trainer. Providing accurate feedback on the correct use of the inhaler and breathing technique, the spacer is ergonomically designed inhaler spacer designed to look exactly the same as your normal asthma inhaler, facilitating ease of use of any metered dose inhaler on the market.

The spacer contains two sensors, an air quality sensor which measures the medicine dose, and an airflow sensor for inhalation detection. A single LED is used to provide feedback - prompting the user when to breathe to optimize the intake of medication.

The AsthmAway app

The AsthmAway app allows the spacer to be utilised to it's full functionality. It records a number of key metrics, providing feedback whilst in training mode, helping train the user to safely and effectively use the inhaler.

The app also provides a full dosing and performance history - enabling patients to share data with parents and doctors to review performance over time.
